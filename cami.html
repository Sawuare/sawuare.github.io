Canadians Against Mass Immigration

Canadians Against Mass Immigration (CAMI) is a non-profit organization. It fights for immigration policies that put Canadians first. I founded it in 2023-10.

Our immigration policy proposals

  • No more than 1 in 200 residents may be a study permit holder.
  • No off-campus work for study permit holders.
  • No Temporary Foreign Worker Program.
  • No birthright citizenship for children of non-citizens.
  • No population growth due to immigration.
  • No new permanents residents until 2050.
