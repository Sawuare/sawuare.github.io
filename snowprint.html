Snowprint

Snowprint is a website that lets you create unique snowflake designs for 3D printing and laser cutting. I created it for my Project I and Project II courses while studying computing science at Algonquin College. Snowprint entered the RE/ACTION Showcase and has a virtual poster with more information about it, including a video and some images.
