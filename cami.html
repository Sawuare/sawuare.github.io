Canadians Against Mass Immigration

Canadians Against Mass Immigration (CAMI) is a non-profit organization. It fights for immigration policies that put Canadians first. I founded it in 2023-10.

Our immigration policy proposals

  • Abolish the Temporary Foreign Worker Program.
  • Limit the number of study permit holders to 200,000.
  • Limit the number of new permanent residents per year to 50,000.
  • Require that at least 1 parent be a Canadian citizen or permanent resident for their future children to become automatic Canadian citizens.
