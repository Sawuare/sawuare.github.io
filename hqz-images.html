HQZ Images

Introduction

HQZ images are mathematical images that I discovered. Some of them are in the gallery below. A description of them is in this documentation.

Software

The software related to the images is free and available in this repository. The main programs are hqz-fb (for viewing images) and hqz-png (for saving images).

Check out hqz-web for an interactive demo of the images.

Gallery

The images here are 512 pixels in width and height. The numbers in the captions denote the painter and Q-value used, respectively.
