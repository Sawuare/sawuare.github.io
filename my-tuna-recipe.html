My Tuna Recipe

This recipe is easy, tasty, and healthy (or at least, not unhealthy).

Ingredients

IngredientAmountBenefit
Solid light tuna in oil1 can ≈ 104 gProtein and fat
White pita bread2 loaves ≈ 76 gCarbohydrate
Yellow mustard3 tspSourness
Hot sauce1 tspPain
Dried chopped onion2 tspCrunchiness
Dried chopped parsley1 tspColor

Preparation

  1. Open the can, drain the oil, then empty the tuna in a plate.
  2. Add the mustard, sauce, and onion over the tuna, then mix them slightly.
  3. Sprinkle the parsley over the tuna.

The tuna and mustard should have enough salt. If not, add salt or more mustard.

Serving suggestion

Serve with the bread on the side.
