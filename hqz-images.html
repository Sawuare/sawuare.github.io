HQZ Images

Introduction

HQZ images are mathematical images that I discovered around 2019. Some of them are in the gallery below. A description of them is in this documentation.

Software

My program hqz-fb displays HQZ images and makes it easy to explore them. My other program hqz-png writes the images, including the ones in the gallery. These programs and more are free and available in this repository.

Check out hqz-web for an interactive demo of the images.

Gallery

The images here are 512 pixels in width and height. The captions show the painter and Q-argument used, respectively.
